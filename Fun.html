My Story

Kaggle.com has been one of the main resources that are helping me to learn data science. So, after reading books, whatching tutorials on YouTube, and learning from other various sources i then go to kaggle.com to consolidate my learning. On Kaggle, I learn from their well structured mini-courses and get certified. I also benefit a lot from the huge data Science community on Kaggle as well as the many datasets available. And yes, on 19 January, 2024, i got promoted from a Kaggle Novice to a Contributer. One day hopefully, I will be a grandmaster! But I guess the greatest learning will take place during the execution of projects and for that, there are no certificates to show for it. Visit my Portfolio Projects and see for yourself!
